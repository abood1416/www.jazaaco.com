Concrete Cooling Solutions

Watch Our Cooling Solutions in Action

Concrete Cooling Solutions

Jazaaco our partner established in the year 1996, we are leading organization engaged in Design, manufacturing, exporting and supplying wide range of products like Flake Ice Plant, Tube Ice Plant, Chilled water plant, and more.

Cooling Technologies

  • Flake Ice Plants: Production capacity from 5 to 100 tons per day, delivering ice at -7°C for rapid concrete temperature reduction.
  • Chilled Water Systems: Closed-loop cooling with plate heat exchangers to maintain precise concrete mixing temperatures.
  • Tube Ice Plant: A specialized facility that produces clear, cylindrical ice with a hole in the middle by freezing water inside vertical stainless steel tubes.
  • Automatic Ice Handling: A mechanized system of screw conveyors, belts, and pneumatic blowers that moves ice from production to storage or bagging without manual labor.
  • Ice Storage Room: An insulated, refrigerated space designed to hold large quantities of ice at sub-zero temperatures to prevent melting and clumping.
  • Air Cooling System: A network of fans and evaporators that circulates chilled air to maintain a stable environment and prevent heat gain within the facility.
